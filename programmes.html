Nitexa

NITEXA 2018 is an initiative of the Young Innovators of Nigeria (YIN) which brings together relevant stakeholders from the private, public, non-governmental sectors and the international Technology community. The Nigeria innovation technology exhibition and awards annual international Technology summit tagged NITEXA 2018 will be the first National flagship development program by young innovators of Nigeria. NITEXA 2018 will focus on developing policies, and creating a platform for stakeholders to discuss the actualisation of Goals 2,9 and 12 of the sustainable development Goals which are:

  • Zero Hunger
  • Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure
  • Responsible Production and Consumption

Innovations are catalyst to national development and Technology is vital ingredient for innovation to make meaningful impact to any nation that is willing to compete among global leading economy. ICT is the enabler to ignite other sectoral development.

Benue-YIN Hub Initiative

Vision

To increase output productivity of rural agriculture and also provide a platform for farmers to sell their products on our portal.

Overall Objective

To provide information to rural farmers to eliminate waste and increase output coupled with a need to sell their product without stress.

The Benue-YIN Hub initiative is an idea that was bore out of the need to increase rural agricultural productivity through capacity building and empowerment of extension workers. The idea is also to create an electronic portal where famers will be able to connect to prospective buyers of their products without stress and also have a physical prototype farm in Otukpo-Ogobia where we carry out most of the e-agric projects and practical field training for the extension workers. The Hub will also serve as an accelerator for developing youths in specified areas of ICT (software and hardware).

Customised capacity building program (for cooperative farmers, rural community farmers) that will enlighten farmers on better ways to do increase productivity and reduce wastage through access to up-to-date information. The major goal is to increase livelihood of rural farmers and also empower graduates in trained agricultural extension work.

Fabrication of farm equipment’s like ( e-silos, e-dryers ,harvesters) and hiring to local farmers and selling to clients that can afford to buy the equipment for larger farms

Young Innovators Coder Dojo

YIN-CoderDojo is a Nationwide movement of free, volunteer-led, community-based programming clubs for young people. Anyone aged seven to seventeen can visit a Dojo where they can learn to code, build a website, create an app or a game, and explore technology in an informal, creative, and social environment. The project will be based on the international organisation ( CodeDojo ) program, we aim to secure a partnership with the organisation for the project.

The YIN-CoderDojo movement believes that an understanding of programming languages is increasingly important in the modern world, that it’s both better and easier to learn these skills early, and that nobody should be denied the opportunity to do so.

To that end, YIN intends to build a national network of free, volunteer-led, community-based programming clubs for young people. Anyone aged seven to seventeen can visit a Dojo where they can learn to code, build a website, create an app or a game, and explore technology in an informal, creative, and social environment. The YIN-CoderDojo Movement has a focus on peer learning, youth mentoring and self-led learning. We aim to help young people realise that they can build a positive future through coding and community help.

Annual YIN Girls Code Workshop

The aim of the Girls Coding is to encourage more women to pursue technical careers by empowering them with vital knowledge to facilitate their success.
